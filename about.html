About CourseHub

We teach by building, not by lecturing

CourseHub started as a small internal training program and grew into a project-based platform for people who learn best by shipping real things.

Our story

From a training room to a full platform

CourseHub began as an internal bootcamp for a small team who wanted a faster way to onboard new developers. The project-first format worked so well that we opened it up — first to a handful of learners, now to thousands.

Every track is still written and reviewed by working developers and designers, not just recorded once and left untouched.
